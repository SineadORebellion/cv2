Bio

I was born and raised in a suburb north of Chicago. Close to where Home Alone and the Breakfast Club were filmed. For college, I attended Indiana University in Bloomington, Indiana. I sailed around the world while living and studying aboard a ship for a Semester at Sea Program. After college, I spent a few years in Evanston, IL. I got married, had a baby, and then after the crash of 2008; after my husband and I had lost our jobs and both of my parents' companies went out of business, we moved to Albany Park to save money.

Situated on the North side of Chicago, Albany Park is one of the most diverse neighborhoods in the country. We thought we'd be here for a year as we regrouped, but after 10 years, we're still here! My husband works remotely for a big tech company. I work along side him from our dining room that doubles as our home office. We have a clever daughter who is 10 (going on 30) and an energetic son who is 8.

We purchased a nearby bungalow and we're doing a full gut rehab. It's exciting to make this little home our own! My husband has a background in construction engineering and I have a love of interior design and small space planning. We've edited the floorplan, and I'm selecting all of the finishings from solid wood kitchen cabinets to a fishing basket light fixture I purchased from Belgium.
