Bio

I was born and raised in a suburb north of Chicago; close to where Home Alone and The Breakfast Club were filmed. After college, I spent a few years in Evanston, IL, working for a mortgage company. I purchased a vintage condo on the lake and renovated it. I turned the dining room into a 2nd bedroom and rented it out. After the crash of 2008, after my husband and I both lost our jobs, we moved to Albany Park to save money.

Situated on the North side of Chicago, Albany Park is one of the most diverse neighborhoods in the country. We thought we'd be here for a year as we regrouped, but after 10 years, we're still here! My husband works remotely for a big tech company. I work alongside him from our dining room that doubles as our home office. We have a clever daughter who is 10 and an energetic son who is 8. As our family has grown, we've created attractive, built-in storage solutions to meet our needs and make our space feel larger.

We recently purchased a nearby bungalow. We're doing a full gut rehab! My husband has a background in construction engineering and I have a love of interior design and small space planning. We've torn everything out and changed the floorplan. I'm selecting all of the finishings from solid wood kitchen cabinets to a giant, fishing-basket, light fixture I plan to hang over the dining table. I can't wait to share photos of it all coming together!
